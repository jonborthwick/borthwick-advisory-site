About

The connective tissue
between functions.

Jon Borthwick has spent 17+ years inside high-growth SaaS companies — not advising from the outside, but running pricing, GTM, and commercial strategy as a senior operator. His edge is the connective tissue between functions: turning product, finance, and go-to-market into a unified growth system.

At LogMeIn, he spent 13 years scaling from $25M to $1B ARR — leading pricing strategy on the GoTo merger (tripling ARR overnight), driving the $125M LastPass acquisition, and growing LastPass ARR from $12M to $40M post-close. At Cordance, he was Employee #4 — building the commercial model for a PE-backed SaaS roll-up that went from $0 to $40M ARR in 18 months across 10 acquisitions, including migrating an entire portfolio from perpetual licenses to SaaS. At Rapid7, he led monetization strategy, platform packaging, and ARR uplift for a publicly traded MDR provider.

The advisory is built on that experience — compressed into an AI that can answer most questions instantly, with Jon in the loop on everything that actually matters.
